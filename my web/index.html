About Us

Welcome to REMEKVIN Restaurant, where we bring the vibrant flavors of Sri Lanka to your plate. Our journey began with a passion for showcasing the diverse and rich culinary heritage of Sri Lankan cuisine.

At REMEKVIN, we pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations to create authentic dishes that tantalize the taste buds and evoke a sense of nostalgia.

Our team of talented chefs, led by Chef Ranjan, Chef Priya, and Chef Dilshan, infuse each dish with creativity, expertise, and a deep understanding of flavor profiles to ensure an unforgettable dining experience for our guests.

Whether you're craving the comforting warmth of a fragrant curry, the crispiness of a hopper, or the tanginess of a pol sambol, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey with us and savor the essence of Sri Lankan cuisine at REMEKVIN Restaurant.

Most Popular Dishes

Hoppers

Hoppers are a Sri Lankan specialty made from fermented rice batter and coconut milk, served with savory toppings.

Kottu Roti

Kottu Roti is a popular street food in Sri Lanka, made with shredded flatbread, vegetables, and a choice of meat or eggs.

Lamprais

Lamprais is a Dutch-influenced Sri Lankan dish consisting of rice cooked in stock, accompanied by a variety of side dishes and wrapped in banana leaves.

Pol Sambol

Pol Sambol is a spicy coconut relish made with grated coconut, chili peppers, onions, and lime juice, often served as a condiment with rice dishes.

Fish Ambul Thiyal

Fish Ambul Thiyal is a tangy and spicy fish curry from Sri Lanka, made with tamarind, goraka (Malabar tamarind), and a blend of spices.

Polos Curry

Polos Curry is a flavorful Sri Lankan dish made with young jackfruit simmered in a coconut milk gravy with aromatic spices.
